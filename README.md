# Movies_Analysis_Project
## Overview
Analysis of top 20 movies to identify trends in ratings, revenue an genres.

## Tools Used
- Python(Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Power BI

## Key Findings
- Avatar is the highest grossing movie (2923M)
- The Shawshank Redemption has the highest rating (9.3)
- Sci-Fi is the most profitable genre (5774M)
- Strong correlation (0.81) between budget and revenue
- High rating does NOT mean high revenue
- 2009 was the best year for box office revenue
- Drama has the highest average rating (8.95)
